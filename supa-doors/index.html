    SupaDoors

    SUPADOORS was founded in San Antonio, Texas over 20 years ago with a view to finding a practical and economical solution to the challenges experienced with painting Pine and Hardwood stile and rail doors.

    The founders of SUPADOORS recognized, that given the superior stability, paintability, and machinability of Medium Density Fiberboard, this sustainable product offered the perfect medium to manufacture better quality and more economical paint grade doors that the industry had traditionally experienced.
